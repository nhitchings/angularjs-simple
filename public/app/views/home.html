What's with the name?

When I was in highschool and someone told me their name I would immediately process it, and repeat it to them backwards. Mainly for fun, and to keep my brain thinking differently. Everyone started calling me Ekalb, which is my name backwards. I ran with it.

My name is Blake Detherage.

Ever since I can remember I have been drawing. I'm a designer, a painter, an illustrator, a photographer, and an artist. I enjoy working with clients and creative teams, and helping them bring out their best work.

I'm passionate about taking concepts, developing them, and delivering them into this world. Whatever it may be.
